Emergency

Our Emergency Department Provides Immediate, High-Quality Medical Care For Urgent And Life-Threatening Conditions. Equipped With Advanced Technology And A Skilled Medical Team, We Ensure Rapid Response And Expert Attention 24/7.

Laboratory

Our Laboratory Delivers Accurate And Timely Diagnostic Testing To Support Effective Treatment Decisions. We Maintain The Highest Standards Of Precision, Hygiene, And Efficiency Across All Lab Services.

Surgeries

El-Safwa Polyclinic Offers A Wide Range Of Minor And Specialized Surgical Procedures In A Safe, Sterile Environment. Our Experienced Surgeons And Medical Staff Prioritize Patient Safety, Comfort, And Fast Recovery.

Outpatient Clinics

Our Outpatient Clinics Provide Comprehensive Consultations And Follow-Up Care Across All Medical Specialties. Patients Receive Personalized Attention And Expert Diagnosis Without The Need For Hospital Admission.

Radiology

Using Advanced Imaging Technology, Our Radiology Department Offers Accurate Diagnostics Including X-Rays, Ultrasounds, And Other Imaging Services — Helping Doctors Detect And Treat Conditions With Precision.

Home Visits

We Bring Quality Healthcare To Your Doorstep Through Our Home Visit Service. Our Medical Professionals Provide Consultations, Checkups, And Treatments In The Comfort And Safety Of Your Home.
